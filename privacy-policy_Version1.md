# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 March 2026

Code N Curry ("the App") is provided by Code N Curry Restaurant ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the App to browse our menu and place food orders.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide to Us

- **Google account information**: When you sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information from your Google account (such as your name, email address, and profile picture) through Firebase Authentication. We do not receive or store your Google account password.
- **Contact information**: such as your phone number so we can contact you about your order if needed.
- **Delivery address**: such as your street address, city, and postal code so we can deliver your food.
- **Order information**: items ordered, special instructions, order time, and order history.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

The App may process limited technical information necessary for operation and security (for example, device/OS information and basic logs) when you use the App.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Allow you to sign in and use the App.
- Process your food orders and provide customer support.
- Deliver orders to the address you provide.
- Maintain your account and order history.
- Protect the App and users from abuse and security issues.
- Comply with legal obligations where required.

We do **not** sell your personal information.

---

## 3. Firebase and Google Sign‑In (Third-Party Service)

We use **Google Firebase** to operate key parts of the App, including:

- **Firebase Authentication** for Google Sign‑In.
- (If applicable) **Firebase Database / Firestore** to store your profile, delivery address, and order history.

We do **not** use analytics services (including Firebase Analytics) in the App at this time.

Firebase and Google may process certain information as part of providing their services (such as authentication and infrastructure). They do so according to their own privacy and security practices.

---

## 4. Sharing of Information

We share your information only as needed to provide the service:

- **With our restaurant and delivery staff** to prepare and deliver your order (for example, your name, phone number, order details, and delivery address).
- **With service providers** (such as Firebase) who help us operate the App. These providers are permitted to process data only to provide services to us and not for their own marketing purposes.
- **For legal reasons** if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

We do **not** share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to:

- Provide the App and fulfill your orders,
- Maintain your account and order history,
- Meet legal and accounting requirements (if applicable).

You may request deletion of your account and associated personal information by contacting us (see Contact Us), subject to any legal requirements to retain certain records.

---

## 6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of online transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## 8. Your Rights and Choices

You may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information depending on your region. To request access or deletion, please contact us using the details below.

You may also revoke the App’s access from your Google account settings if you no longer want to use the App.

---

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date at the top when changes are made.
